FAQs

FAQS

Why is www.shortlistchefs.com.au so unique?

As specialists in chef recruitment for partner venues across Australia, we understand the challenges that employers can face. We provide an up to date active resource for businesses to view what we have to offer and decide if it’s the right fit for them. Shortlist Chefs only showcases around 5% of our live candidate base to bring the best on the market directly to you. With a huge pool of talent to draw from, we ensure that our platform is updated daily so there is no ‘dead wood’. If you see a chef’s profile on the Shortlist, you can be sure that they are actively looking for their next career move and are ready to make it happen. Our team will also send you new profiles periodically and optional alerts if a new match to your requirements has been uploaded. This allows you the opportunity to speak to great chefs before everyone else and put you in charge of the process.

What does this cost me?

We currently do not charge anything for clients to sign up for our service and you can view as many profiles as you like without incurring a charge. A placement fee will only be charged if you successfully hire a chef from the Shortlist. We appreciate that every business is different and each requirements is unique. As such, Shortlist Chefs does not apply a one size fits all fee structure and we offer a ‘tailor made’ service quotation based on the key criteria and offering you the best value on the market. There are no hidden charges and our business model is based around placing lots of great chefs and bringing new talent to the Shortlist all year round. Not only do you get a cost-effective service, a payment plan may also be offered to keep cashflow in check also. This is subject to certain conditions.

What Happens After I Hire A Chef?

Shortlist Chefs has an exceptionally low turnover rate when it comes to placements and we believe that is due to the selectiveness of our process and the fact that you drive the process and chose the best fit for your business. Because of this, our chefs and partner venues tend to live happily ever after, however we do offer exceptional peace of mind as standard with ALL placements. If there are any issues or the relationship isn’t working out, we offer an extended 6 month guaranteed period. You can feel secure knowing that we will work with you if a replacement chef is required and you can be confident that we have the expertise and candidate access to find an alternative for you quickly and efficiently.

What Do You Need from Me?

Shortlist Chefs provides exciting and genuine career opportunities for our talent base so we just need to be sure that you can offer just that. As well as looking at candidates’ experience and skills carefully, our team will require some basic company information and a detailed job description as we rely heavily on profile matching as the core of our service. Our online team will liaise with you to walk you through the process and ensure that it’s as seamless as possible. We approach all relationships with integrity and transparency and have the utmost respect for clients, candidates and the wonderful industry that we all work in.

I’m Not Too Sure What I Need… Can You Help?

Yes, the team @ Shortlist Chefs is highly experienced in chef recruitment and we partner with recruitment professionals that understand the industry and have extensive experience in this space. team will assist you by offering an insight into recent placements in your area and perhaps a couple of different options that may work well for your business. Our team will also work With years of experience and a passion for what we do, we are dedicated to helping employers and job seekers connect, and will work hard to help you find the right person for every role. If you are open to suggestion or a little unsure as to what might be ‘out there’ our recruitment with you to keep on top of the budget whilst never compromising on the talent you will be offered. There’s always going to be a few different options and our highly trained team will just help you make the most of it.

Shortlist Chefs is a simple, highly efficient platform that showcases the best culinary talent for you to review and potentially hire. We remove all the effort and energy out of the hiring process by only showing you quality, available candidates that you can assess for your requirements.

How much do you charge?

It’s completely FREE to join Shortlist Chefs and set up an account and view talent. You can look at as many profiles as you like and we only charge a conversion fee in the event of you successfully hiring a chef from www.shortlistchefs.com.au

What sort of Chefs are on the site?

Shortlist Chefs’ marketplace is focused on providing candidates who are actively seeking their next permanent career moved and are ready for a long-term commitment.

How can my company join Shortlist Chefs?

Go to www.Shortlistchefs.com/signmeup and follow the quick and easy process and we will issue you with unique login details so you can start viewing profiles straight away. Our commitment is to deliver you high-quality and active chefs whilst providing talent with exciting, real job opportunities. Therefore, we ask both parties to go through an application process before joining www.shortlistchefs.com.au Our team will be in touch to discuss your requirements and assist with your requirements and using the site within 48 hours.

Why do I see different candidates every week?

Shortlist Chefs is an active marketplace of qualified, available talent. It is not a database. As such, we have new talent joining Shortlist Chefs every week who are actively seeking their next career move. Candidates will stay on the Shortlist Chefs platform for four weeks or until they secure a new opportunity.

Where do you source your talent from?

Finding talent is hard and as such we use several ways to attract top candidates to our platform. We leverage several online channels as well as proprietary sourcing tools and techniques to identify potential candidates who are ready to look for their next opportunity. Additionally, we use more traditional sourcing methodologies such as networking, word of mouth and referrals.

A candidate rejected my initial personalised opportunity. What should I do?

If a candidate rejects your initial interview request, we’ll work with you to identify the potential factors (for example, salary or job scope) and give you the chance to revise your opportunity to meet the candidate’s expectations, if appropriate for both parties. There are also new active talent joining the marketplace every week, and there may just be the perfect one for your company.

Can I contact the talent on Shortlist Chefs outside of the marketplace?

No. All candidates should be contacted via the Shortlist Chefs platform only to ensure that both you and the candidate get the best experience possible. Any breach of our terms of use may result in your company being removed from our marketplace.

Is there a discount if I hire multiple people from Shortlist Chefs?

We have a few options. Best talk to your account manager once you've signed up.
