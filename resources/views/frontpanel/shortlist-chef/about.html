ABOUT US

What is Shortlist Chefs?

www.shortlistchefs.com.au is a talent-sourcing platform which allows you to view profiles of experienced chefs and hire the best fit for your business. Our focus is on providing chefs looking for long term permanent career moves and often willing to relocate anywhere in Australia. We select only the best chefs to be included on the shortlist and we leverage from a huge database offered by our recruitment partners to give you the best selection. You will only ever see profiles of experienced chefs who can truly add value to any food operation. Shortlist Chefs makes it easy for anybody to hire the very best chefs and we specialise in providing staff for ‘hard to fill’ roles in regional and remote locations as well as in central locations.

Shortlist Chefs gives you access to a pool of highly qualified candidates who are actively looking for their next career move.

We are not a ‘one size fits all’ service. Our team appreciates that each requirement is different, and we offer tailor made pricing which is dependent on several different factors. We even offer payment plans in certain circumstances.

Signing up to view profiles is completely FREE once you have been approved. You do not pay anything unless you successfully hire a chef from Shortlist Chefs, and they join your business team.
